About Brandon Lavack  CPT, LMT, NTP

Brandon is one of the more accredited trainers in his trade, fueled by a desire to learn and apply his experience to help clients attain lasting results. Brandon has a B.S. from St. Lawrence University, where he majored in Psychology and was a competitive runner. He is a Professional Trainer who is based out of the popular Beverly Athletic Club in Beverly, Massachusetts. His many certifications serve as testament to his passionate pursuit to be an exceptional trainer and fitness partner.
